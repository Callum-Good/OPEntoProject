Meet the team at Ento

Ento is a new startup company based in Dunedin that is aiming to make human insect consumption mainstream in New Zealand.

We stand on the edge of an environmental and nutritional tipping point, with more people eating more meat more often. Collectively, we are faced with the problem of providing more food for more people with the same resources. To add to all that, we have increasing urban populations and a planet struggling under the weight of an agricultural sector using massive quantities of water, land, and substantial greenhouse gas emissions.
